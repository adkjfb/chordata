Tasmanian Devil

Lifespan: 5 years

Top speed: 8-10 mph

Weight: 9-26 lbs

Diet: Wallabies, wombats, small mammals, birds, carrion

Predators: Eagles, owls, and quolls as juveniles

Range: Tasmania

Conservation Status: Endangered

Fun Fact: Tasmanian devils have the highest bite force compared to size of any mammal, with a bite force of 553 newtons. They are the largest extant carnivorous marsupial.


Fun Fact: Tasmanian devils store fat in their tails.

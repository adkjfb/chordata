Whale Shark

Lifespan: 130 years

Top speed: 3 mph

Weight: 30,000-45,000 pounds

Diet: Phytoplankton, zooplankton, small fish

Predators: Orcas and sharks, including blue sharks and great white sharks, will sometimes hunt juveniles, but adults have no natural predators

Range: Tropical oceans, generally between 30˚N and 35˚S

Conservation Status: Endangered

Fun Fact: Each whale shark has a unique pattern of spots and stripes.


Fun Fact: Whale sharks migrate, and can travel up to 5,000 miles each year.

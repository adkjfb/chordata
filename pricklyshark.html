Prickly Shark

Lifespan: Unknown

Weight: Up to 500 lbs

Diet: Small sharks, rays, cephalopods

Predators: Orcas and larger sharks, mostly for juvenile prickly sharks

Range: Tropical and subtropical Pacific ocean

Conservation Status: Data Deficient

Fun Fact: Prickly sharks get their name from the thorn-like denticles on their skin.


Fun Fact: Prickly sharks are ovoviviparous, meaning they produce eggs that hatch inside the mother and give live birth. They have a litter size of up to 114 pups.

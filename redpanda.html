Red Panda

Lifespan: 8-10 years in the wild, up to 15 years in captivity

Top speed: 24 mph

Weight: 8-15 lbs

Diet: Bamboo shoots and leaf tips make up 95% of their diet, they also eat grasses, roots, and insects occasionally

Predators: Yellow-throated martens and snow leopards

Range: Eastern Himalayas and Southwest China

Conservation Status: Endangered

Fun Fact: Red pandas are the original pandas, as giant pandas were not discovered by western science until 44 years after red pandas.


Fun Fact: Red pandas have a false thumb that they use to grasp bamboo; it is actually a modified wrist bone.
